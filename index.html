About

Understanding your journey through personalized therapeutic care

As a licensed therapist with over a decade of experience, I specialize in creating warm, supportive environments where healing can flourish. My approach combines traditional therapeutic techniques with mindfulness practices and innovative tools.

I believe that every individual deserves a space where they feel heard, understood, and empowered to navigate life's challenges. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or seeking personal growth, I'm here to support your unique journey.

My practice integrates evidence-based therapies including CBT, DBT, and mindfulness-based interventions, tailored to meet your specific needs and goals.
